RMB + drag - move window
Always on top - P or pin icon toggle

Space - pause/resume (peep the coffee symbol when the session is on pause)
Left/Right - prev / next
F11 - fullscreen
Esc - exit fullscreen

G or rainbow toggle - black & white
R or grid buttton - rule of thirds grid
F or horizontal arrows button  - flip horizontal
V or vertical arrows button - flip vertical

+ or = — extend current image timer
Countdown for per image timer, appears visible when runs out
Progress bar for session limit (peep the alarm clock symbol when time runs out)
Drag edges to resize the window

H - help/info
Click anywhere to close info
